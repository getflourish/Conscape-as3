A project by Jeremias Volker, Florian Schulz and Julian Stahnke at the University of Applied Sciences Potsdam in the class ‘Urbane Ebenen’ by Till Nagel 2010/2011.

Conscape

Exploring Berlin’s live music scene on a multi-touch table.

Conscape let’s you explore Berlin’s concert landscape on a multi-touch table. You can see what kind of music is played where. You can browse all of Berlin’s gigs and how many people attended them. You can spot trends over time. And surprisingly, you’ll learn that contrary to popular belief, it’s not all electro all the time.
